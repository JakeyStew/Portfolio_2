After spending a couple years developing and practising my Web Development skills and started my own journey developing outside of a commercial setting, I decided to dive back into my main hobby developing video games.

Following the excellent Jonathan Weinberger and his courses on Udemy I was able to produce the following game. This game is a trial product of diving back into the Unity game engine, the course allowed me to learn the process of design, development and deployment.
